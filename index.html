About Me

I build computational methods for materials and process systems, combining scientific computing, multiscale modeling, and machine learning to deliver practical, transferable tools. My work spans polymer networks, ceramics, battery systems, and X-ray diagnostics, with an emphasis on robust algorithms and interpretable modeling.
